Netflix

Netflix is a video streaming service that offers both original and TV shows, films, documentaries, and kid's content.
It was founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph as a spin-off of their company Blockbuster Video.
The concept behind Netflix is to provide customers with DVD-quality streaming for a monthly fee (currently $9.99 USD per month).

Photoshop(PS)

Photoshop is a tool that helps you to create high-quality images. It has a lot of tools and can be used for many purposes.
The most common uses are graphic design, photo-editing, video editing, web design, and many more.
It also has several programs like Photoshop Elements, Photoshop CS6, CC, Paint Shop Pro X7, Adobe Camera Raw (ACR), Dreamweaver, etc.
There are also other products like Lightroom which is an image management program developed by Adobe Systems.

Google Chrome

Google Chrome is a web browser developed by Google company. It was first released in 2008 and is available for multiple platforms including Android, iOS, macOS, Windows, Linux, and Chrome OS.
Chrome is used to access the Internet and search the Web. Users can also sync their bookmarks with Google Bookmarks and use other online services like Gmail or YouTube using Chrome as an interface.
